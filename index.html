Our content

Mathematics

The purest science and the one that rules them all, also known as "students' nightmare". However, you will be impressed with how Maths can be interesting and tackle a lot of real world problems.

Physics

Physics is the study of the world and univers-astrophysics-, starting from classical/newtonian mechanics to modern physics you will be learning how the world around you works.

Chemistry

Chemistry is the study of the matters, how they are formed and how they interact with each other. You will go in a journey starting from the periodic table of elements to organic chemistry.

Biology

Biology is the study of life, you will learn about your body: how it works, how it grows up and how it maintains energy, you will also study about all kind of species from humans to unicellulars
